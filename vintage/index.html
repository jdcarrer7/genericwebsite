About Us

SparkleClean Services is a family-owned and operated cleaning business that has been serving our community for over 15 years. What started as a small two-person operation has grown into one of the area's most trusted names in home and office cleaning.

Every member of our team is carefully screened, trained and supervised. We treat your home or office with the same care and respect that we would our own. We use safe, effective cleaning products and pay close attention to every little detail - because we know it's the details that count!

We are fully bonded and insured, and all of our work is backed by our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee.
